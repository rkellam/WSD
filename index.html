Brewtopia at a Glance

We served our first cup of coffee in 2005 as a pocket espresso bar in Northwest Washington, D.C., and opened a small-batch roastery the following year. Our tongue-in-cheek advertisements in the local papers touted world-class neighborhood coffee to go, and the tasty brew gained an instant following. As of 2012, we're still utterly captivated by coffee craft.

We've been fortunate to draw high-profile praise for our obsessive approach to sourcing, roasting, and preparing artisan coffee. A Washington Post article, Coffee's New Wave Hits Town, featured Brewtopia as a leader in the artisanal coffee movement. The Baltimore Sun newspaper labeled us the ultimate no-nonsense coffee spot, while the New York Post wrote, hands, shoulders above any cup of coffee we've tasted, anywhere.

Locally, Brewtopia has earned a reputation for community-building, historic preservation, small business health, and philanthropy. Our cafes are dynamic meeting spots that have revitalized commercial and neighborhood spaces. We're continually motivated by our fanatical customers and friends who make a make a place for us in their daily lives. Viva la neighborhood.
